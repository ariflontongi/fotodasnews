You don't need clues from the mysterious "A" to figure this one out: Ashley Benson is a married woman. On Wednesday, a source confirmed to People that the Pretty Little Liars alumna and oil heir Brandon Davis recently tied the knot. Per Entertainment Tonight, Benson's mother Shannon posted a photo to Instagram (which is no longer available) that seemingly captured Ashley and Davis wearing wedding bands.

Although Benson and Davis have kept their relationship fairly private, the actress has offered up a few snippets of their romance over the last year.
