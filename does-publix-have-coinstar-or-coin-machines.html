How To Use Coin Machines

These devices are designed to be very quick and easy to use. However, the process can be a bit confusing if you have never done it before. 

First, you should make sure that all the coins you want to convert are completely clean. If they are covered with dirt or lint, the machine may have trouble converting them. Then you need to feed the coins into the device steadily so that it does not become clogged. 

Get a receipt and receive your money!

Once you have deposited all the coins, they will be counted. Then choose the type of conversion you would like to make. You will receive a receipt, which you need to take to the customer service desk. A store employee will then hand over your cash. 

Fees and limits

You will be charged between 9% and 10% of your total transaction for using the service. This means that if you place $100 in change into a device, you will only receive $90 back. If you have a lot of change to convert, the machine saves you the hassle of counting it out by hand. And you can convert as much change as you want as there are no limits in place. 

Payout options

You can exchange your small change for dollar bills. When you have finished adding your coins, the machine will provide a receipt stating the amount. You can then redeem your receipt for cash at the store customer service counter. Unfortunately, this is the only available payout option.
