Thinking of ditching the desert dust of Mesa for a Tucson adventure? Excellent choice, my friend! The Straight and Narrow (or Highway, Rather)

Let's cut to the chase, because who needs suspense when there's sunshine and cacti to be seen? The distance between Mesa and Tucson depends on the route you choose, but generally speaking, you're looking at a cruise between 114 and 118 miles. That translates to a breezy 1 hour and 50-ish minutes of driving, perfect for catching up on podcasts or belting out your favorite road trip anthems.
