Boosie Badazz discovered new alleged paperwork on Terrance “Gangsta” Williams. This morning, Boosie took to Instagram where he shared a photo of the paperwork that reportedly states Williams helped police solve over 40 homicides. The alleged paperwork states, “The New Orleans Cold Case Homicide division sent a letter to the USAD of the Eastern District Of Lousiana (“EDLA”) in which it outlined Williams’ cooperation in 40 cold case homicides.” It continues, “In an attempt to reduce his life sentence, Williams closed 40 cold case homicides.”

However, if you look a bit deeper into the paperwork, it reads that Williams admitted to several of the murders. Still, Boosie had a field day with the information. “SO HE TOLD ON 40 DEAD PEOPLE,” Boosie wrote. “HELL NA HE TOLD ON THE WHOLE NEW ORLEANS WTF he look like #mastersplinter on this pic lol SAMMY THE BULL HAS BEEN OUT DONE BY A N***A FROM LOUISIANA,” he added. “THIS AINT BLACK HISTORY THIS RAT HISTORY WTF EVERYBODY SHOULD RUN WHEN U SEE THIS MAN," he added.

Afterward, Terrance Williams shared a video of Boosie claiming to have filed a police report against the police, as well as a civil suit, over stolen jewelry.
