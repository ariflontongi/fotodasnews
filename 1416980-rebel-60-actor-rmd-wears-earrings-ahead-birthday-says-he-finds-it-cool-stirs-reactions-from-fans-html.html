Popular Nollywood actor, Richard Mofe Damijo aka RMD recently debuted his new look on social media to celebrate ahead of his 60th birthday.

The movie star took to his verified Instagram page to share series of photos of himself wearing earrings and explained that he had always wanted to do it.

According to him, he finds wearing the accessory cool and that he wanted to do it 10 years ago when he was turning 50. RMD said he only decided against it because he didn’t know how the people of Delta state would feel about a commissioner wearing earrings.
