Calvin Harris and Dua Lipa Deliver Electric Banger &quot;One Kiss&quot;

August 28, 2024
post

Everybody loves a good dance song. World renown DJ Calvin Harris unleased the PartyNextDoor collaboration "Nuh Ready Nuh Ready" earlier this year, and now the Scottish producer is back for another addicting offering. Today (April 6), Harris offers the world "One Kiss," which features Dua Lipa. The talented British singer's gorgeous vocals are immediately recognizable over the 90's style house instrumental. Harris teased the track on Monday (April 2), and the hypnotic dance banger will undoubtedly be in heavy rotation at clubs this Spring.

The new single finds Lipa singing about the bliss of love, and how just one simple lip to lip interaction can trigger the most intense lust. The strength of her performance drives the concept of "One Kiss" home immaculately.
