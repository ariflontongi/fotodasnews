Does Maurices offer free shipping?

Yes, Maurices offers free shipping to its customers who spend over $50. For orders under this amount, standard shipping will cost $6.95. Alternatively, customers who own a Maurices store credit card will receive free shipping with their every order. 

Does Maurice's offer student discount?

Maurices does not currently offer any form of student discount. However, this may change in the future. Alternatively, Maurices does offer a 10% military discount for members of the military and their families. 

What is the Maurices Rewards Program?

Join the MyMaurices Rewards Program to receive a $5 reward coupon for every 100 points you earn, as well as receiving an exclusive birthday gift, bonus points days, access to the latest promo codes, and even a welcome offer when you first join. 

Can you order in-store or curb-side pick up from Maurices?

Maurices offers a 'Doorside Pickup' service for a convenient shopping experience. Simply place your order online and choose the store location to pick it up from. 

What is Maurices returns policy?

Maurices returns policy states that returns can be made at any time, providing the item is in good condition, with all original tags and packaging intact and with no sign of wear or use. A returns fee of $5.95 will be deducted from your refund. We would recommend referring to the Returns page of the Maurices website for more information.
