A young Nigerian man, @fadelosky, has made a video to show how he travelled by air from Lagos to Abuja to get a partner for his dog.

In a clip he shared online, he showed when the dog was packed for the flight. After thorough searching, the man revealed that he could not get a male dog for his pet in Lagos.

Bullmastiff gets mating partner in Abuja

After getting to Abuja, he met with a partner he found for his bullmastiff. After the mating was done, they both went back to Lagos.

"I did three times. It was a week trip, I got to Abj, allowed the girl to rest then started the mating the following day, I mated her thrice, if we mate today, we would skip tomorrow then repeat mating a day after, all together, 3 times. That trip was very expensive though, my stay at the hotel alone cost me a lot."
