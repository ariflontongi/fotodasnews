Ross Store Hours

When it comes to shopping at Ross Stores, it’s important to know their operating hours, especially if you’re planning a visit to one of their locations. Regular Store Hours

Ross Stores is open seven days a week, with most locations open from Monday to Saturday from 9:00 a.m. to 9:30 p.m., and on Sundays from 10:00 a.m. to 8:00 p.m. However, store hours may vary by location, so it’s always a good idea to check the hours of your closest store before heading out.

Holiday Store Hours

During the holiday season, Ross Stores extends its regular store hours to accommodate the increased traffic. This means that stores may open earlier and close later than usual, so it’s important to check with your local store for their holiday hours. Additionally, Ross Stores is closed on Easter Sunday, Thanksgiving Day, and Christmas Day, so plan accordingly.

Senior Hours

Ross Stores offers special senior hours for shoppers aged 55 and older on Tuesdays and Thursdays from 9:00 a.m. to 10:00 a.m. during regular store hours. This is a great opportunity for seniors to shop in a less crowded environment and take advantage of special discounts that are available exclusively for them.

Early Bird Hours

In addition to senior hours, Ross Stores also offers “early bird” hours on Fridays from 8:00 a.m. to 10:00 a.m. These early hours allow shoppers to get a head start on their shopping before the store officially opens to the public. However, not all stores offer early bird hours, so it’s important to check with your local store before planning your shopping trip.
