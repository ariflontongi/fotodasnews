Yungeen Ace &amp; JayDaYoungan Pull Up With The Gang In &quot;Jungle&quot;

August 11, 2024
post

You never know what might happen tomorrow. For Yungeen Ace, he's living every day as if it were his last. In June, Ace was the victim of multiple gunshot wounds, being transported to the hospital in critical condition. After surviving that as well as a jail sentence, he's making the most of his "now or never" attitude by going all in on his music. Earlier this month, he teamed up with JayDaYoungan on "Jungle," a high-energy affair that showcases the best of both rappers. Today, the music video has officially been released.

In the visuals, Yungeen Ace pulls up with his crew, stunting in the streets and reminding us he's the king of the "Jungle." If you haven't heard anything from the young artist, let this be your introduction to his Southern charm.
