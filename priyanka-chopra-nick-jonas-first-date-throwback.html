Priyanka Chopra Posts Throwback Pic of First Date with Nick Jonas

August 10, 2024
post
  • Priyanka Chopra and Nick Jonas both shared throwback photos over the weekend in honor of the two-year anniversary of their first date.
  • Chopra shared an image of the couple from their first date at Dodger Stadium, while Jonas posted a silly photo of the pair donning cowboy hats.
  • The couple married in two lavish wedding ceremonies in 2018.

Priyanka Chopra and Nick Jonas are reminiscing about the night when it all began.

On Sunday, Chopra shared a sweet throwback post on social media from her and her now-hubby Jonas's first date together. The actress was honoring the two-year anniversary of their initial outting together, which quickly led to their whirlwind romance and two lavish wedding ceremonies.

"2 years ago today we took our very first picture together. Every day since then you have brought me endless joy and happiness. I love you @nickjonas," wrote Chopra above the photo. "Thank you for making our life together so incredible. Here's to many more date nights."

The shared image showcased Chopra and Jonas striking a pose at Dodger Stadium, while both donning navy blue caps for the occasion. Jonas, too, shared a post celebrating the big anny, and hats, it seems, were a running theme for this walk down memory lane. In Jonas's snap, the two are on a different date but again rocking some headware—this time, they sported cowboy hats.

"This beautiful woman and I went on our first date two years ago today. It has been the best two years of my life, and to think I am lucky enough to get to spend the rest of my life with her is such an incredible blessing," wrote Jonas of the sentimental occasion. "I love you babe. Happy two years @priyankachopra."

The couple married in two ceremonies on December 1 and 2, 2018—one was an American-inspired wedding, the other a traditional Hindu ceremony—in Chopra's home country of India. And though they plan to start a family one day, having a baby isn't quite yet in the cards for the married couple. Chopra credited her jam-packed work schedule as the main reason the two haven't taken that next step.

"This year's really packed for me in terms of the work that I'm doing and the work that I've taken on," Chopra recently told Tatler magazine. "But having a family is very important to me and it always has been. It is something that I definitely want to do and I'm hoping that whenever God wills it, at the right, opportune time, it'll happen."
