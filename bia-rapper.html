Bia Rapper (born 16 August 1991 [Age: 29 years]; Real Name: Bianca Miquela Landrau) is a well-known artist, musician, rapper, lyricist, YouTuber, social media face, actress, entrepreneur, and media personality from the United States. She rose to fame for her recent collaboration with the pop star named Nicki Minaj for the song ‘Whole Lotta Money’. 

People like the collaboration of the duo as both are super-talented singers. The official video song was released on July 09, 2021 (Friday). After some time of the release, Nicki and Bia also came live on Instagram to interact with their fans and get feedback about the song. 

Apart from this, Bia has also featured in a reality show named ‘Sisterhood of Hip Hop’ in 2014. She worked in the same show for 2 years. She has released many singles and EPs such as Piñata, Whip It, Safari, For Certain, and many more. 

The list doesn’t end here, she has also made her own channel on YouTube, where she uploads her latest and upcoming songs. Bia has also appeared in many magazines and interviews. In addition to this, she has also launched her own fashion brand. She sells the items of clothing at very reasonable prices and a printed image of herself. 

Bia Rapper’s Biography 

Bia Rapper Wikipedia:- Bianca Miquela Landrau was born on 16 August 1991 in Medford, Massachusetts, United States. Her full real name is Bianca Miquela Landrau. In 2017, she shifted to Los Angeles, California, USA. 

She took admission to Medford High School for her high school graduation. As per Wikipedia, she enrolled herself in a reputed college for further studies but dropped that in the middle as she started taking interest in music and rapping.

Music Career 

After dropping her college studies, Bia started working as a bartender to arrange money for studios. She decided to become a rapper during her college days. In 2014 and 2015, she has also featured in the Oxygen reality television show named ‘Sisterhood of Hip Hop’. She did only 2 seasons of this television show. Let me tell you that she also runs her own online merch, where she provides tops, t-shirts, and masks of her image. 

As a Rapper

According to the reports, her talent was discovered by American rapper Pharrell Williams. She started working with Clifford Joseph Harris Jr. popularly known as T.I. and signed to the music label named ‘I Am Other’. In the same year, she released her first mixtape titled ‘#CholaSeason’. 

Afterward, Bia released many singles such as Hollywood, Same Hands, Whip It, Skate, Cover Girl, and others. Moreover, she has also featured in many songs such as Best on Earth by Russ, Piñata by Vice, Remember Me by Dove Cameron, Facts by Chantel Jeffries, Safari by J Balvin, and many more. 

Let me tell you that Rapper has also released some extended plays, which are more than singles but less than an album. In 2018, she worked on an EP (extended play) named ‘Nice Girls Finish Last: Cuidado’ under the record label RCA. In 2020, she completed and released her other EP named ‘For Certain’ under the record label Epic.
