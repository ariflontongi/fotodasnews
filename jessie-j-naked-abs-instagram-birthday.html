Jessie J Shared A Naked Instagram Selfie To Celebrate Her 33rd Birthday

August 21, 2024
post

Jessie J just turned 33 on March 27, and she decided to celebrate the big day with a naked Instagram selfie, as one does. The post was pretty simple, as far as nude posts go: The singer simply shared an artsy-looking shot of herself standing naked in her bathroom, alongside the caption, "33."

People were pretty impressed in the comments. "Omfg 🔥😍," one fan wrote. "my god how beautiful😍😍😍😍," another said.

The "Flashlight" songstress followed up her birthday post up with some new workout videos in her Instagram Story. In them, she’s crushing it at the gym doing…just about everything. There’s Jessie doing leg lifts with an exercise band, using the stair climber with her hair blowing everywhere, doing a quad workout with another exercise band, doing pull-ups with TRX bands, working through sit-ups, and more. In one clip, she’s using a rower and singing, "I hate…the rower."

The "Bang Bang" singer has made it super clear in the past that she goes hard during her workouts. "When I come out of the gym, I’m blotchy red and sweaty, but I feel good. It’s about the longevity of your life, not how you look in the morning," she told HuffPost in 2017.

She also opened up to Women’s Health UK about her commitment to working out. "[Exercising] really makes me feel positive," she told the publication. "It releases endorphins—and I never really used to believe in that until I started working out. You do really get this sluggish feeling before a workout where you’re like, ‘Oh, I don’t want to do it,’ and you might feel a little low. But you do a workout, and it gives you that pickup. It makes me feel alive."
