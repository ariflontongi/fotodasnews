Career

Iba Jamal‘s musical odyssey ignited during his teenage years, as his enthusiasm for crafting lyrics burgeoned into an enchanting desire to sing them aloud. His preferred languages of expression became the melodious Yoruba dialect and the rhythmic pidgin, and his impromptu performances within the schoolyard and neighborhood encapsulated the heart of his creative spirit.

In the nascent phase of his career, Iba Jamal sought refuge in a nearby studio, where he etched his early singles into soundwaves. As his reputation as a vocalist flourished, he found himself gracing local stages and concerts, enveloped by the spirited ambiance of Lagos.

The year 2019 shone a spotlight on Iba Jamal as he unleashed the resonant hit, Baba, enlisting the talents of Zinoleesky and Mohbad. This brilliant collaboration served as his passage into the limelight, making his presence felt across the industry.

Over five years, Iba Jamal harmonized with luminaries like Zinoleesky and Mohbad, melding his artistry with theirs. Furthermore, his affiliation with Obimzy Properties Ltd. as a brand ambassador underscores his position as a cultural envoy, intertwining his aura with a brand’s narrative.
