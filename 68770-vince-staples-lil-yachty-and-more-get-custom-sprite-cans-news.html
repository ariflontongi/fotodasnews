Vince Staples, Lil Yachty &amp; More Get Custom Sprite Cans

August 15, 2024
post

Sprite continues to embrace the hip-hop community, and the company has teamed up with Vince Staples, Lil Yachty, D.R.A.M., Vic Mensa, Kamaiyah & Kap G to launch a new series of custom cans and bottles. In Staples' case, his can will be adorned with his notorious line "Bitch you thirsty, please grab a Sprite."As you might have guessed, "bitch" will be omitted. 

Yachty's can will feature his lyric "cause it get cold like Minnesota," and the Sprite-loving words of each respective artist will be immortalized in can-form, at least until they are recycled and disappear into obscurity.  The limited edition run will extend to 16-ounce Sprite cans and 20-ounce Sprite bottles, available between June 26th till August 28th.
